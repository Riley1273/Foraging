Foraging

Project Description

Inspired by my interest in mushroom foraging, this project attempts to replicate the feeling of searching for mushrooms in nature. Within the forest are 4 different types of mushrooms, although you may not always be able to find all 4 of them. As you search for the mushrooms, you can move around to different parts in the forest. Upon finding a mushroom, hovering over it will display information about the mushroom you have found. All 4 types of mushrooms in this forest are poisonous, representing the potential dangers of hunting for mushrooms in real life.

Instructions

1) Hold down your mouse and drag over the canvas to move through the forest.
2) If you find a mushroom, hover your mouse over it (no need to press), to display an informational page about the mushroom.
3) To return to the forest, simply move your mouse away from the mushroom.
